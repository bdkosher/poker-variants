# Great Gig in the Sky

Texas Hold 'em where a King (a.k.a. "Great Gig") dealt in the community cards (a.k.a. "The Sky") causes the card after the King to be wild. If a King is dealt in the River, then Kings are wild. 

## Variations

1. If a King is dealt in the river, the dealer turns over the top card of the deck: that card becomes wild.
